About

I am a theoretical nuclear physicist working on the phase structure of Quantum Chromodynamics (QCD) from first principles. My research focuses on chiral phase transitions, critical phenomena, and functional renormalization group methods.

Research Interests

  • QCD phase diagram at finite temperature and density
  • Chiral phase transitions in the chiral limit
  • Critical scaling and universality classes
  • Functional Renormalization Group (FRG)
  • Quark-meson models and effective theories
